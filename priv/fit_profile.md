agent-native SaaS (raised a pre-seed from South Park Commons).

- Platform engineering, infrastructure, developer tooling, AI/agent products
- Product engineering at companies where AI tooling is central, not bolted on
- Founding/early engineer at well-funded startups is interesting when the
  problem space is agents, infra, or developer experience
